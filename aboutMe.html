My Mission

To bridge human needs with innovative solutions through thoughtful design, creating intuitive and impactful experiences that enrich lives.

02.

My Goal

To continuously evolve as a design leader, driving user-centric product development that translates complex challenges into elegant, effective, and emotionally resonant solutions.

03.

Why Me

I blend a deep passion for understanding users with a versatile skill set in product, UI/UX, and project management. My journey demonstrates a proactive learning approach, cross-functional collaboration, and a proven ability to bring designs from concept to tangible, user-loved realities.

Hi! I'm Owen, a designer with a passion for problem-solving and a drive to explore all that life has to offer. I believe that good design makes life smoother and more enjoyable.

I love design not just because it's fun, but because it empowers us to help people. I enjoy putting myself in the user's shoes, thinking about their real needs, and then using design to turn those needs into reality.

  • Product Design: From concept ideation to prototype development, creating products that meet user needs and market expectations.
  • UI/UX Design: Designing user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces, and applying front-end knowledge to ensure smooth implementation.
  • Project Management: Leading teams to complete projects and ensuring that design concepts are successfully realized.

I'm a lifelong learner and someone who embraces challenges. I believe that through continuous learning and growth, I can bring more meaningful designs to the world.

Some Thoughts

For me, design is a process of problem-solving and value creation. I enjoy using design to simplify complex things and make life more efficient. The process of self-teaching front-end skills has also allowed me to better understand the connection between design and development, enabling me to make more comprehensive considerations in my design work.

Keen to connect?

I'm currently exploring new remote opportunities and design roles. If you're in Taiwan, I'd love to meet up for coffee, or we can always schedule a call. Let's chat!
